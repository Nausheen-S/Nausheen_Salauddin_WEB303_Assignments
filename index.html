Shaw Mendes Fan Page

  • Shawn Peter Raul Mendes, is a songwriter and singer and was born in Pickering, Ontario to Karen Mendes, and Manuel Mendes. His mother is a real estate agent and father a businessman. He has a younger sister named Aaliyah. He graduated in 2016 from Pine Ridge Secondary School and was voted as one of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2018 by Times.

  • At the age of 14, Mendes learned to play guitar by watching YouTube videos, and was soon posting cover videos on YouTube. In 2013, he almost instantaneously received 10,000 likes on Yelp for cover from Justin Bieber's "As Long as You Love Me" where he first gained recognition from the music world, and by August 2014, he was the third most-followed musician on Vine. Mendes released his first single, "Life of the Party" in the June of 2014 and his first album, EP in July, 2014. Apart from singing, he is also an actor and he made his acting debut on The CW's “The 100” third-season premiere. Some of his other roles include:

    1. 13 SOCAN Awards
    2. 10 MTV Europe Music Awards
    3. 8 Juno Awards
    4. 8 iHeartRadio MMVAs
    5. 2 American Music Awards

  • Mendes has been open about his struggles with anxiety, which he disclosed publicly through "In My Blood", a track from his third studio album. He declared that he had been undergoing therapy in order to help him deal with the mental health condition.

  • Mendes created the Mexico Earthquake Relief Fund in conjunction with the American Red Cross and donated $100,000 towards relief efforts. In 2018, Mendes worked with Omaze to raise funds through donations to support the WE Schools program, a movement aiming to support youth through educational services and mentorship.

  • Mendes signed with Wilhelmina Models in 2016. In June 2017, Mendes walked the runway during the "Emporio Armani Spring 2018" show held in Milan, Italy.
